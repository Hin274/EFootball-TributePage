Features

  • Free-to-play however, there are in-game purchases available.
  • Compatible with the following systems: PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, Windows , Steam , iOS, Android
  • Supports cross-gen platform matches. Where Playstation 5 users can play against Playstation 4 users.
  • Authentic Team mode. This lets you play matches against AI or with other users using real world club teams offline. You can also play other users online to gain rewards to help build your Dream Team.
  • Dream Team mode. This lets you create your very own club team where you can sign players and managers to fit your ideal playing style. The players and managers can be signed using the in game currency earned when playing the game, or using coins purchased from the EFootball shop.

Why do I like this game?

I have played both FIFA and PES(before rebranded to EFootball) between 2007 to 2014 however, I was more fond of playing PES as I felt more engaged due to the football simulation being very close to the sport in reality regardless of the lack of licenses compared to FIFA.

Free game

Efootball 2022 is a free game on any system whereas before it was a paid game all be it this series is slightly limited game modes due to it being a new adventure for Konami where they have made a decision that any other game modes such as the popular Master League will be a downloadable paid content.

Great rewards and free items

I think Konami are very generous with this aspect. They reward their users with free items such as in game currency, coins or even a free player just by logging in to the game daily or if the game was down for maintenance for updates.

Simplified tactics

They offer the template for each style of play for you to choose from, so you could just quickly pick your style and get into a game. Therefore you don't have to go into much depth tweaking each individiual setting, wasting a lot of time setting up your team.
